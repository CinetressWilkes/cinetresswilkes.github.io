Project Excel - Bike Sales

Project Summary

  • In this project i took the raw dataset and cleaned it and prepared it for analysis.
  • The purpose of my analysis was to derive answers to the following questions:
  • Does the average income of someone affect if someone buys or does not buy a bike?
  • What is the average income per purchase by gender?
  • Does the commute distance affect whether or not someone decides to buy a bike?
  • Does the age range of the customer affect whether or not they're more likely to purchase a bike?

Data Cleaning

  • The first step I took in data cleaning was removing any duplicates.
  • The next step was making the data easier to read. I started in the Gender Column changing : M --> to Male, F --> to Female. In the Marital Status Column I changed : M --> to Married and S to --> Single.

Data Analysis

Answering "Does the average income of someone affect if someone buys or does not buy a bike?" People who earn more income on average tend to buy a bike. " What is the average income per purchase by gender?" Men make more money in general averaging $58,063.

Answering "Does the commute distance affect whether or not someone decides to buy a bike? " If someone has a longer distance to work, they're less likely to purchase a bike.

Answering "Does the age range of the customer affect whether or not they're more likely to purchase a bike?" On average people in the Old age bracket are more likely to purchase a bike.
